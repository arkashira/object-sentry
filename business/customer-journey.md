# Customer Journey Map
## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Developer or researcher encounters a project requiring object detection, image processing, or tracking capabilities, leading to a search for suitable solutions.
* **Friction Points**: Difficulty in finding efficient and accurate computer vision platforms, lack of knowledge about available solutions.
* **User Emotions**: Frustration, curiosity.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Introduction to object-sentry through targeted advertising, blog posts, or word-of-mouth, highlighting its efficiency and accuracy.
* **Metric**: Website traffic, social media engagement (e.g., 500 new visitors, 20% increase in Twitter followers).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Potential customer discovers object-sentry and begins evaluating its features and pricing.
* **Friction Points**: Comparing object-sentry with other computer vision platforms, concerns about integration and compatibility.
* **User Emotions**: Interest, skepticism.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear documentation, tutorials, and demos showcasing object-sentry's capabilities, responsive customer support.
* **Metric**: Number of demo requests (e.g., 30 demo requests), time spent on documentation pages (e.g., average 10 minutes).

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: Potential customer decides to try object-sentry, either through a free trial or a small-scale pilot project.
* **Friction Points**: Technical difficulties during setup, uncertainty about optimal usage.
* **User Emotions**: Excitement, apprehension.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless onboarding process, timely and helpful support, impressive performance in pilot projects.
* **Metric**: Trial conversion rate (e.g., 25% of trial users convert to paid), customer satisfaction (CSAT) score (e.g., 85%).

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Customer decides to adopt object-sentry for their project or organization.
* **Friction Points**: Integration challenges, potential bugs or performance issues.
* **User Emotions**: Relief, anticipation.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Smooth integration, proactive issue resolution, regular updates with new features.
* **Metric**: Customer retention rate (e.g., 90% retained after 6 months), net promoter score (NPS) (e.g., +30).

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Customer achieves success with object-sentry and considers expanding its use to more projects or teams.
* **Friction Points**: Concerns about scalability, cost of expanded usage.
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, ambition.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Customized solutions for large-scale deployments, flexible pricing models, continuous innovation.
* **Metric**: Upsell/cross-sell rate (e.g., 20% of customers expand their usage), customer lifetime value (CLV) growth (e.g., 15% increase).
